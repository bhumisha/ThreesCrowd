Welcome to Three's A Crowd!

How To Play:

The deck will automatically be shuffled when you start a game. Click on a player card to turn the card over. The computer will then automatically take a turn.

Rules:

There are five rounds. The first one to get three cards of the same suit wins each round. If you win the most rounds, you beat the computer!
